NutriMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This page explains how NutriMind handles your data. NutriMind is designed to keep your information under your control.

English Deutsch Español Français Italiano

English

NutriMind stores your entries primarily on your device. The app does not upload your personal diary data to our own servers.

Data used by the app

  • Profile information you enter, such as gender, birth year, height, weight, goals and settings.
  • Diary entries such as weight, body fat, calories, macros, steps, active calories, training minutes and body measurements.
  • Optional Apple Health data, but only if you explicitly grant permission.
  • Optional purchase status for premium features via Apple In-App Purchase.

Apple Health

If you allow Health access, NutriMind reads selected health data to fill in your entries more automatically. Health data is only used inside the app to display and analyze your progress.

Data sharing

NutriMind does not sell your data and does not send your diary data to third parties. Payments and purchase validation are handled by Apple.

Local storage

Your app data is stored locally on your device. If you export your data yourself, you decide where that exported file is stored.

Your control

You can edit, export, import or delete your data inside the app. You can also revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@nutrimind.app
